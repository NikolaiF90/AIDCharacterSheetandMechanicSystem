Ordinance Library

A curated collection of ready-to-use Ordinances. Drop them into your scenario and start playing.

How to use these. Copy the Name and Entry into a new story card. Set Type to Custom and type Ordinance in the field that appears. Add the name as a trigger keyword. That's it — the Ordinance is live in your scenario.

Combat

Quick Strike
DAMAGING No target required: No
A lightning-fast strike delivered before the opponent can react. Prioritizes speed over power — relies on the element of surprise rather than brute force.
Submitted by: PrinceF90
Flashing Shadow
DAMAGING No target required: No
An explosive burst of movement — closes distance instantly, evades into the target's blind spot, and delivers a concentrated strike before they can reorient. High risk, high reward. If the roll fails, the caller is left exposed and vulnerable.
Submitted by: PrinceF90
Incapacitating Juggler
DAMAGING No target required: No
A sustained sequence of punches, kicks, and joint locks designed to overwhelm and immobilize. Ends in a pin or takedown. Damage is cumulative — each strike builds on the last. Difficult to escape once the sequence begins.
Submitted by: PrinceF90

Utility

Unwavering Focus
NONDAMAGING No target required: Yes
The character clears their mind and centers themselves completely. Distractions fade. Fear subsides. For the next action, the character acts with absolute clarity — their next roll gains advantage regardless of circumstances.
Submitted by: PrinceF90
